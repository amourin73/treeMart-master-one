Planting Tips for Current Weather

    {% if weather.temperature < 10 %}
  • Cold Weather Planting: Choose cold-hardy species that can withstand frost
  • Plant in sheltered locations to protect from wind
  • Consider evergreens that maintain foliage year-round
  • Mulch heavily to protect roots from freezing temperatures
    • {% elif weather.temperature < 20 %}
  • Mild Weather Planting: Ideal time for planting most tree species
  • Ensure proper soil preparation before planting
  • Water regularly until the tree is established
  • Consider native species for best adaptation
    • {% elif weather.temperature < 30 %}
  • Warm Weather Planting: Select drought-tolerant species that can handle heat
  • Water deeply but less frequently to encourage deep roots
  • Provide afternoon shade for young trees
  • Apply mulch to retain soil moisture
    • {% else %}
  • Hot Weather Planting: Choose heat-resistant species
  • Plant in early morning or late afternoon to reduce stress
  • Water frequently during establishment period
  • Use shade cloth for newly planted trees
    • {% endif %} {% if weather.condition.lower == 'rain' %}
  • Rainy Conditions: Excellent for planting as soil is moist
  • Ensure proper drainage to prevent waterlogging
    • {% elif weather.condition.lower == 'sunny' %}
  • Sunny Conditions: Protect newly planted trees from intense sun
  • Water more frequently during sunny periods
    • {% endif %}
{% endif %}
